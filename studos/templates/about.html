About Us

Stu-Dos was created with one simple belief: Every student deserves support, clarity, and confidence.

Who We Are

We are a student-focused assistance platform built to help learners handle the real challenges of academic life — documentation, applications, skills, and career decisions — without stress or confusion.

At Stu-Dos, we don’t see students as customers. We see them as dreamers, learners, and future professionals who just need the right guidance at the right time.

Why Stu-Dos Exists

In today’s world, students are expected to manage everything on their own. But reality is different. Many students feel confused, fear making mistakes, lack guidance, and don’t know whom to trust.

Stu-Dos was born to fill this gap. We exist to simplify processes, reduce pressure, and stand beside students when they need help the most.

A Message from the Founder

"Stu-Dos was not born out of comfort. It was born out of struggle — struggles I have personally faced and struggles I have witnessed in countless students... Stu-Dos is a reflection of real student experiences, created to ensure that no student feels lost while trying to move forward."

— Siva Sai Krishna Sankurathri
Founder & CEO, Stu-Dos
